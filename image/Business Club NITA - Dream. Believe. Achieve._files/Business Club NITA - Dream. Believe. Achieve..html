Business Club, NIT Agartala, is a student-run organization that provides a collaborative environment for students to learn about and explore the world of business.

The club is dedicated to fostering growth, innovation, and professional excellence. Through workshops, mentorship programs, competitions, and collaborative projects, we equip our members with the tools they need to excel in their careers and make impactful contributions to the business community.
